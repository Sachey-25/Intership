Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is a model which automates the learning process of machine.

Artificial intelligence (AI) refers to technology that enables computers and machines to simulate human learning, comprehension, problem solving, decision making, creativity, and autonomy. It is a branch of computer science that aims to build machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. AI allows machines to recognize speech, make decisions, and identify patterns. It is the ability of a computer or robot to perform tasks associated with human reasoning. The concept of AI has evolved over decades of research and technological advancements.
